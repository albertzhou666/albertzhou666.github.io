## Standard

* Linux like name: lower case with dash
  
  * java-coding-standard.pdf

* Data and time as ISO8601
  
  * iceland-20220706.jpg

* Two or three sequence number starts with 01 or 001
  
  * 新疆-202207-001.jpg

* Start with data or sequence number if needed
  
  * 01-docs
  
  * 02-pics
  
  * 202207-新疆
